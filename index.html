The Plan

Since we’re still quite a few months from the wedding we don’t have all the details planned out yet, but the key things are:

  • Saturday 9th September – wedding ceremony at 1pm at Priston Mill, followed by general merriment, wedding breakfast, and a jig into the night.
  • Sunday 10th September - all guests invited to a late morning stroll near Bath, followed by a pub lunch.

Location

Both the ceremony and reception will be held at the Watermill at Priston Mill, which is about 6 miles south-west of Bath.

Accommodation

There are some lovely places to stay in and around Bath, ranging from spa hotels to b&bs, self catering cottages, or even camping or the youth hostel!

Some of the options around the centre of Bath that we’ve looked at are below. TripAdvisor, as well as the Priston Mill website, have many more options.

B&Bs

We and some of the wedding party will be staying at Duke's Guest House. They have more rooms available and we'd be delighted if some of you are are able to join us (note they have a two-night minimum stay at weekends, as do Apsley House and the Arylington below).

These are four well-recommended and very comfy looking guesthouses, all in and around Bath:

  • Apsley House
  • The Arylington
  • Harington’s Hotel
  • Brooks Guest House – slightly cheaper than the other B&Bs, and one-night stays are fine.

Hotels

  • The Hilton Bath City – comfortable and reliable hotel in the centre of Bath.
  • Express By Holiday Inn – slightly cheaper option, also in the centre of the city.
  • Ston Easton Park – very beautiful but also pricey spa hotel.

Other options

  • Bath Youth Hostel – set in a gorgeous building, with private and en-suite rooms as well as dorms.
  • AirBnB has a plethora of options across the city and further out, with private rooms from around £60 in the city centre.
For those of you who'd prefer not to stay in Bath itself, there are also options in the countryside near to Priston Mill (the Priston Mill website is a good place to start).
