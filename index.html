Accommodation

There are some lovely places to stay in and around Bath, ranging from spa hotels to b&bs, self catering cottages, or even camping or the youth hostel! Note it does tend to get busy at weekends (and apparently we’re clashing with a 200 year Jane Austen Festival…!).

Some of the options around the centre of Bath which we’ve look at are below. TripAdvisor, as well as the Priston Mill website, have many more options.
